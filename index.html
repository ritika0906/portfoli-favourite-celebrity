Background

Taapsee Pannu who was born on 1 August 1987 is an Indian actress who works primarily in Hindi, Telugu and Tamil-language films. After completing her graduation, Pannu worked as a software engineer. She became a full-time model after she auditioned and was selected for Channel V's talent show Get Gorgeous, which eventually led her to acting. Taapsee Pannu belongs to a Jat Sikh Family. Her father, Dilmohan Singh Pannu, is a real estate agent and her mother, Nirmaljeet Pannu is a homemaker. She has a younger sister, Shagun Pannu. Taapsee was in a relationship with the Tamil actor, Mahat Raghavendra for two years.

Awards

Taapsee Pannu has won 4 awards - Filmfare Award in 2021 for "Best Actor In A Leading Role (Female)" for movie Thappad, Filmfare Award in 2020 for "Critics Best Actor In Leading Role (Female)" for movie Saand Ki Aankh, Zee Cine Award in 2020 "Best Actor - Female" for movie Badla and IIFA in 2017 for "Woman Of The Year".
